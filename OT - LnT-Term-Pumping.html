Pumping Motion

Movement of a piece along the axis of rotation (the Z axis).

Pump Cutting on the MDF Rose Engine

The second one shows pumping on an MDF Rose Engine (which Pat Miller made). This is a very innovative approach that Pat made, using the rosette designed to move the object in a rocking motion, and converting motion that into a pumping action.
