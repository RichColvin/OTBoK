Organizations for Ornamental Turners

Ornamental Turners Group of Australia (OTGA) is a group of ornamental turners down under. It is based in Australia and New Zeland.

Ornamental Turners International (OTI) is a chapter of the American Association of Woodturners (AAW). It is based in the United States of America.

The Society of Ornamental Turners (SOT) exists to encourage, develop, and promote the study and practice of the art of ornamental turning. It is based in the United Kingdom.
